Our Story

Founded with a vision to democratize financial planning, Mishika Finvest Pvt Ltd has been serving clients for over 22 years. We started as a small advisory firm and have grown into a comprehensive financial services company, always keeping our clients' best interests at heart.

Our journey began with a simple belief: everyone deserves access to professional financial advice and quality investment products. Today, we're proud to have helped thousands of families secure their financial future through systematic planning and disciplined investing.

As a registered AMFI distributor and IRDAI licensed insurance advisor, we maintain the highest standards of professionalism and transparency in all our dealings.
